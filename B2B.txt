The software functions right from booking to boarding the flight.

Current plan/features:
1. Book a flight
2. Pay using the app using UPI, Netbanking for the booking.
3. Database of the passenger
4. Database of the flights
5. Self Check in and baggage check in
6. Notify about the flight status
7. Display status of all the current flights
    a. Boarding
    b. Departed
    c. Cancelled
    d. Final Call
    e. Delayed
8. Displays baggage lane on landing

Future plan/features:
1. Make users wallet
    a. Keep a record
    b. Add or remove money
    c. Transfer money directly through wallet
2. Shopping Mart 
3. Displays travel history
4. Details of the flight crew
